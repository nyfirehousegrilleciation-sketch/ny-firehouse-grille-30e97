Frequently Asked Questions About Wings Menu

How much do your wings cost, and do you offer any special deals or combos?

We frequently run daily specials, including Wing Night on Wednesdays where you can get select flavors for just $0.75 per wing (dine-in only). Combo deals with fries and a drink are also available—just ask your server for current promotions.
How quickly can I get a large wing order for a party or event?
For large party orders—such as 50 wings or more—we recommend calling at least 24 hours in advance, especially for weekends or big game days. Smaller takeout orders (under 30 wings) can usually be ready within 20-30 minutes. For same-day large orders, give us a call and we’ll do our best to accommodate based on our kitchen’s capacity.
Is NY Firehouse Grille licensed to serve alcohol, and are there any local restrictions I should know about?
Yes, we are fully licensed to serve beer, wine, and cocktails on-site at our Peekskill location. Guests must be 21 or older to order alcoholic beverages, and a valid photo ID is required. Peekskill city ordinances prohibit outside alcohol on the premises, and all alcohol must be consumed within the restaurant or on our designated patio.
What is the process for ordering wings with different sauces or heat levels?
You can customize your wing order by choosing from over 30 house-made sauces, ranging from mild to extremely hot. Just let your server or the staff know which flavors and heat levels you'd like—mixing and matching is welcome! For takeout, you can specify your preferences when you order by phone or online, and we label each flavor for easy identification.
