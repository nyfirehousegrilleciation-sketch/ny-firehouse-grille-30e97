Frequently Asked Questions About Private Events

How far in advance should we book a private event at NY Firehouse Grille in Peekskill?
We recommend booking your private event at least 4-6 weeks in advance, especially for weekend dates or during peak seasons like summer and the holidays. This gives us enough time to work with you on menu selections, room setup, and special requests. For smaller gatherings, we may be able to accommodate you with shorter notice, but availability is best the earlier you reserve.
What are the pricing options for private parties or events, and is there a minimum spend?
Our private event pricing is based on the size of your group, menu selection, and day of the week. For most events, we require a minimum spend, which typically starts at $500 for weekday luncheons and $1,000 for weekend evenings. We offer buffet, plated, and cocktail-style menu packages, and we're happy to customize options to fit your budget. All pricing includes use of our private event space, basic setup, and staff.
Is NY Firehouse Grille licensed to serve alcohol at private events, and can we bring our own drinks?
Yes, we are fully licensed to serve beer, wine, and cocktails at all private events held at our Peekskill location. Due to New York State liquor laws, we cannot allow outside alcohol to be brought in. However, we offer several bar packages, including open bar, cash bar, and drink tickets, and can work with you to create a selection that fits your group's preferences.
What is the process for planning a private event at NY Firehouse Grille?
Once you submit an inquiry, our event coordinator will reach out to discuss your date, group size, and event needs. We’ll help you choose the best menu and beverage options, finalize a floor plan, and review any special requirements. After you confirm your booking with a deposit, we’ll keep in touch to finalize details and ensure everything is ready for your event day. On the day of your event, our team will handle setup, service, and cleanup so you can enjoy your celebration.
Do you offer any guarantees if something goes wrong with our event or if we have to cancel?
We strive to make every event memorable and seamless. If there are any issues during your event, please let our staff know right away so we can address them on the spot. For cancellations, our policy allows for a full refund of your deposit if you cancel at least 14 days prior to your event date. For cancellations within 14 days, the deposit is non-refundable, but we’re happy to work with you to reschedule if possible. Full details are provided in your event agreement.
